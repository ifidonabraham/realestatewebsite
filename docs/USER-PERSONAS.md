# User Personas

The Real Estate Finder serves three core user groups. Below are their typical needs, goals, and behaviors.

---

## 1. The Buyer/Renter (Seeking a Home)
**Name:** Emily Chen
**Goal:** Find a safe and affordable 2-bedroom apartment near her new job.
**Needs:**
- Easy-to-use search filters (price, bedrooms, location).
- A way to contact agents directly without having to call.
- A place to save properties they like.
**Frustrations:**
- Seeing outdated or unavailable listings.
- Slow responses from agents.
- Complicated sign-up flows.

---

## 2. The Seller/Landlord (Listing Property)
**Name:** Marcus Johnson
**Goal:** Quickly list his vacant property and find a reliable tenant.
**Needs:**
- A simple form to upload property photos and descriptions.
- A dashboard to track how many people have viewed his listing.
- Direct messaging from interested buyers/renters.
**Frustrations:**
- Dealing with unqualified leads.
- Inability to easily edit his own listings once they are live.

---

## 3. The Real Estate Agent (Business Manager)
**Name:** Sarah Miller
**Goal:** Manage a large portfolio of properties and convert inquiries into sales.
**Needs:**
- A central dashboard to manage multiple listings and messages.
- Verified profiles to build trust with potential clients.
- Quick tools to update property availability and price.
**Frustrations:**
- Mixing personal and professional messages.
- Manual data entry across multiple platforms.
- No central place to track client interactions.
